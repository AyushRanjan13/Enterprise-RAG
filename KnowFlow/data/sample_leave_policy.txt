COMPANY LEAVE POLICY

Effective Date: January 2024

1. ANNUAL LEAVE
   Full-time employees are entitled to 24 paid leave days per year. This includes:
   - Regular weekday leave (Monday-Friday)
   - Long weekends (if aligned with public holidays)
   - Carry-forward allowed up to 5 days per year

   Part-time employees receive pro-rata leave based on hours worked.
   New employees receive 15 days after completing 6 months with the company.

2. SICK LEAVE
   All employees have 10 paid sick leave days per year.
   - No medical certificate required for 1-2 consecutive days
   - Medical certificate mandatory for 3+ consecutive days
   - Unused sick leave does NOT carry forward to next year
   - Sick leave cannot be encashed

3. SPECIAL LEAVE
   
   Maternity Leave:
   - 6 months paid maternity leave for birth mothers
   - Must be taken within 12 months of delivery
   - Partial work-from-home allowed after 3 months
   
   Paternity Leave:
   - 2 weeks paid paternity leave
   - To be taken within 6 months of child birth
   
   Bereavement Leave:
   - 3 days paid leave for death of immediate family
   - Immediate family includes: spouse, child, parent, sibling
   - Extended family: 1 day with manager approval

4. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
   - Company observes 15 public holidays per year
   - Holiday calendar provided at year-start
   - Holiday falls on weekend: not compensated

5. LEAVE REQUEST PROCESS
   - Submit request 2 weeks in advance (except emergency)
   - Use HR Portal for submission
   - Manager approval required
   - HR final confirmation within 48 hours
   - Emergency leave: notify manager same day

6. ENCASHMENT POLICY
   - Only annual leave can be encashed
   - Maximum 5 days per year
   - Encashment at last drawn salary
   - No encashment for unused sick/special leave

7. ATTENDANCE IMPLICATIONS
   - Unauthorized absence: disciplinary action
   - If leave exceeds entitlement: salary deduction + loss of pay
   - Excessive leave requests: subject to review

For questions, contact HR Department: hr@company.com
