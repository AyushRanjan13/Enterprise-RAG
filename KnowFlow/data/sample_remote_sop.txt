REMOTE WORK STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE

Version: 2.0
Last Updated: January 2024

1. OVERVIEW
   This SOP defines guidelines for remote work (work from home / WFH) to ensure 
   productivity, security, and employee well-being.

2. ELIGIBILITY

   Who can work remotely:
   - All permanent employees
   - Contract employees (after 3 months)
   - Excludes: Operations, Facilities, Security staff
   
   Requirements:
   - Minimum 3 months tenure
   - Satisfactory performance rating
   - Manager approval required
   - Stable internet connection

3. REMOTE WORK SCHEDULE

   Frequency:
   - 2-3 days per week (flexible with manager)
   - Fixed or rotating schedule can be agreed
   - Upto 2 weeks continuous for important projects

   Core Hours:
   - 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM (IST) - Must be available
   - All meetings scheduled within core hours
   - Team coordination calls: 11:00 AM daily
   - Project stand-ups: as per team schedule

   After Hours:
   - No expectation to work after 6:00 PM
   - On-call rotations: separate policy applies
   - Weekend work: subject to compensation policy

4. EQUIPMENT & SETUP

   Company Provided:
   - Laptop (MacBook Pro / Dell XPS provided)
   - External monitor (optional, upon request)
   - Keyboard, mouse, docking station
   - Webcam and microphone (if needed)
   - VPN access credentials

   Employee Responsibility:
   - Maintain equipment in good condition
   - Return equipment within 1 week of termination
   - Use only company laptop for work
   - Install security updates promptly

   Internet & Utilities:
   - Company reimburses: $50 per month for internet
   - Electricity: personal responsibility
   - Submission: Monthly internet bill to Finance

5. SECURITY REQUIREMENTS

   Mandatory:
   - Use VPN for all company resources
   - Lock screen when away (Windows + L)
   - Use company-provided VPN on personal WiFi
   - Enable two-factor authentication
   - No screen sharing of sensitive data
   - Keep password confidential
   - Report security incidents to IT

   Workspace:
   - Private space preferred
   - No co-working without approval
   - Do not work in public areas
   - Background check for video calls

6. COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES

   Expected Response Time:
   - Slack/Email: Within 2 hours during core hours
   - Urgent: Escalate to manager
   - Out of office: Set auto-responder
   - Update status in Slack hourly

   Tools:
   - Slack: Primary communication
   - Email: Official documents only
   - Video calls: 10 AM - 4 PM only
   - Do not share passwords via email

7. PERFORMANCE EXPECTATIONS

   Same as office:
   - Deliverables on schedule
   - Project participation expected
   - Meetings mandatory
   - Response to urgent requests

   Monitoring:
   - Project management tools track progress
   - No monitoring software installed
   - Periodic check-ins with manager
   - Annual performance review unchanged

8. REPORTING & DOCUMENTATION

   Daily:
   - Log work hours in timesheet
   - Update project status
   - Share blockers or concerns
   
   Weekly:
   - Team sync meeting: Thursdays 11 AM
   - 1-on-1 with manager: Scheduled
   - Status report: Friday end-of-day

9. POLICY VIOLATIONS

   Consequences:
   - 1st violation: Verbal warning
   - 2nd violation: Written warning + suspension of WFH
   - 3rd violation: Disciplinary action up to termination
   
   Examples:
   - Working without VPN
   - Not attending core hours
   - Sharing credentials
   - Working outside policy hours excessively

10. EXCEPTIONS & SPECIAL CASES

    Approved Scenarios:
    - Project launch phase: 5 days/week
    - Client meetings: In-office required
    - Team building: In-office required
    - Technology issues: 1 day office alternative provided

11. BENEFITS OF REMOTE WORK

    For Company:
    - Increased productivity
    - Cost savings on office space
    - Better talent retention
    - Flexible work environment

    For Employee:
    - Reduced commute time
    - Better work-life balance
    - Flexible schedule within core hours
    - Less office distractions

12. CONTACT & ESCALATION

    Questions:
    - Manager: First point of contact
    - HR Department: hr@company.com
    - IT Support: it@company.com
    
    Emergency:
    - Call manager directly
    - HR on-call: Check emergency contact list
